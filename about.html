About

I'm a JavaScript Developer, working with technologies like HTML5, CSS3, Javascript(ES6+), TypeScript, ReactJS, Angular, Webpack, Gulp, Babel, Underscore.js/Lodash, npm, NodeJS, Mocha, ChaiJS, Jest, Enzyme. In my free time I'm experimenting with WebAssembly and Rust.
